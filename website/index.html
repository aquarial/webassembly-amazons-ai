Game of the Amazons

An abstract strategy game with two teams taking turns back and forth. The last team to make a move wins.

On a turn a player clicks one of their pieces to select it, then clicks a location to move it, then clicks a location to place a 'stone' blocker pieces. Pieces and stones move like queens, and cannot move through other pieces/stones.

The entire website will freeze up while the AI is computing. I'm working on that. Be careful to click "Make AI Move" just once because otherwise it will make multiple moves one-after-another.
